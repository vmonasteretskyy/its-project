ADVANCE YOUR CAREER and LEARN NEW SKILLS

Have you thought about joining the ever-growing cosmetic and aesthetic industry? Now is an excellent time to begin. We at Innovation Training Specialists Center are dedicated to teaching you all the needed skills to succeed.

This microneedling/ Collagen Induction Therapy ( CIT) course is created for Physicians, Nurses, Aestheticians and other practitioners who are looking to learn this popular treatment. This course is designed to educate all practitioners with the ability to correctly analyze and assess a client, explain how microneedling works for different skin concerns and understand the proper treatment technique.

Our amazing Microneedling/CIT course is for practitioners of all skill levels! This course offers a comprehensive class ranging from beginner to advanced techniques.
